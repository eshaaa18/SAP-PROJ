## Project Overview
This repository contains the SAP Enterprise Blueprint for Let's Talk About Wonder Pvt. Ltd. (LTAW), a fictional book manufacturing and distribution company.

The project demonstrates SAP ECC 6.0 configuration for:
- Financial Accounting (FI)
- Materials Management (MM)
- Sales and Distribution (SD)

Full Blueprint PDF: [SAP Project](./2305416Proj.pdf)

---

## Company Overview
- Company: Let's Talk About Wonder Pvt. Ltd.  
- Industry: Book Manufacturing & Publishing  
- Headquarters: Bengaluru, India  
- Revenue: INR 280 Crores (approx.)  
- Employees: ~1200

## SAP Modules

### FI — Financial Accounting
- Company Code: LTAW  
- Chart of Accounts: LTCA  
- Fiscal Year: April–March  

### MM — Materials Management
- Purchasing Organization: LTPO  
- Plants: LTBG, LTPN, LTDL, LTMB  
- Material Types: ROH, FERT  

### SD — Sales and Distribution
- Sales Organization: LTSO  
- Distribution Channels: Retail, Wholesale, E-Commerce, Institutional  
- Pricing Procedure: ZLTAW_PP  

---

## Business Processes

### Procure-to-Pay (P2P)
Purchase Requisition → RFQ → Purchase Order → Goods Receipt → Invoice → Payment  

### Order-to-Cash (O2C)
Sales Order → Delivery → Billing → Payment  

---

## Integration
- MM to FI: Inventory and accounting updates  
- SD to FI: Revenue and receivables  
- SD to MM: Stock availability checks
